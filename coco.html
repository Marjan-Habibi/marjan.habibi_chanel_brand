Coco Chanel Perfume, is a classic, luxurious fragrance launched by the house of Chanel in 1984. It was created by the famous perfumer Jacques Polge. The scent is warm, spicy, and elegant, combining floral, oriental, and woody notes. Coco Chanel perfume represents sophistication and boldness, perfect for evening wear and special occasions. Top notes: Coriander, Mandarin.efore Chanel, women’s clothing was often heavy and restrictive, but she introduced styles that were more relaxed, modern, and free.
